Ateliyay

Ateliyay is an app to rent and to rent out creative workspaces.

An AirBnB clone made with Ruby on Rails, HTML5, SASS, Bootstrap, SQL, and Heroku.

BonjourHi

BonjourHi is an app that matches people who want to learn each other's languages in an informal and casual way. The meetup time is split 50/50, half the time teaching, the other half learning.

Made with Ruby on Rails, HTML5, SASS, Bootstrap, SQL, and Heroku.
